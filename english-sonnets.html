[Sonnet Collection]

Catastrophic Longing

A sickly, craggy complexion overcoming your visage,
As I attend to you even in the midst of adversity.
Is it fitting to talk about “timing” as if time is a commodity?
To let your pain break my soul, my village?

Stemming from hatred and a broken image.
My ruined mind, caused by your perifidy,
Filled to the brim with infirmity,
Rest and comfort now a distant privilege.

Needing a pillar is not unique only to you.
Many thoughts I wish to say, but will it be all for nought?
When I just want to love and your pain does not accrue.

Inner demons you hold accountable but is it true?
From reciprocating my love, or is it just your wish to not?
Make me just a part of your past, is that what you're thinking too?

- John Gabriel Tejero

"Us" Or Just "You" And "Me"?

Staying for support, I accompany you on the most bitter of days
Your eyes devoid of life and body not at all spared from lethargy
You push me off with thorns though I want you only to feel my embrace
Do you know you're placing me and us in fear and jeopardy?

Solitary and lonesomeness , is that what you're feeling?
Negatives in that wide spectrum of emotion
Your inner thoughts now revealing.
You are never alone so please go into motion.

This and that you blame, but never yourself
The wounds that you inflict, do you think I alone can suture?
The memories of us, do you just want to put away on a shelf?
The us in the present, do you want just to be “you” and “me” in the future?

Stopping our progress, is it me or is it you?
That’s obstructing this relationship, please give me a clue!

- John Gabriel Tejero

Letting Go

It hurts to be something;
It's worse to be nothing with you.
Healing begins anew,
Leaving behind a lingering sting.

Finding the strengths to bring
A brand new way to renew
A whole new life without you
Yet with each step, memories cling.

There’s no solution; we’ll never last.
Why can’t I let go of this?
Our memories, now remnants of our past.

Let me find my way, please
To mend my wounds in a cast
As my life without you turns into an abyss.

- Alyanna Martina Inocencio

Lost Pathways

Autumn air cold as an icy frozen sea
But with you felt like home and grace
The feeling of hollowness consumes me
Because our sparks faded, like a ghostly trace

Maybe I asked for too much?
Maybe this thing was a masterpiece?
All I asked was your love
I’m still trying to find it, my peace

You kept me hidden like a secret to the world
But I kept you like a sacred oath
‘Till you decided to break me like glass pearl.
Maybe I was foolish to even hope

My heart and mind still reminisce
Our shared bittersweet bliss

- Alyanna Martina Inocencio

Yearning For More

In the corridors of my mind, thoughts of you I wield,
I’ve tried to halt them, as heaven knows.
Must I linger to see how this tale goes,
Longing for the day my wounded heart is healed?

Within my heart’s covert chambers, where desire’s concealed,
Should I embrace my feelings, holding love close?
Or do I hinder them, arresting their growth?
Would you fathom the ache, hidden and sealed?

I let go of the anchor that held love’s sail,
Moving forward knowing you won’t look back.
Acceptance to soothe a heart that yearned.

I’ll meet someone whose love won’t frail,
I’ll find someone whose love won’t lack,
You're not the one, I’ve finally learned.

- Sabria Claire Ko

Loved and Lost You

In days of yore, when I held you close by,
Patience, I should not have provoked.
My ego fueled your heart’s cries,
Now to reclaim your love is a path I cannot evoke.

From my acts of sheer folly, I watched you depart,
A throne of regrets, my soul cannot help but claim.
My negligence, a poison to the heart,
In silent agony, I bear love's blame.

Had I been true, in loving you,
A symphony of joy, our hearts would weave.
But alas, my choices left us both blue,
Now shadows dance, and bitter tears deceive.

At long last, your heart has been unbound,
With another man, you’ll need not frown.

- Sabria Claire Ko

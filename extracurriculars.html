Extra-Curriculars
CLUBS AND ALAS

Canterbury High School also offers a wide variety of extra-curricular activities available for students to enjoy. In order to take a break from academics, or to embrace them even further, students can join up to 3 clubs!
The clubs are divided into three categories: the ACADEMIC CLUBS, SPORTS CLUBS and HOME-ECONOMIC CLUBS

COMPETITIONS AND ACTIVITIES

Aside from clubs, our school also hosts and participates in various competitions from throughout the world. Given below are competitions that are currently accepting participants and challengers.


Clubs and ALA

Academic Clubs

PHYSICS CLUB

The Physics Club is a student-led organization that celebrates the study of physics among its members. Through regular physics sessions, experiments, and competitions, members can enhance their scientific knowledge, improve their analytical skills, and develop their creativity. Join us in measuring, calculating, and understanding our way to physics wonders!
MATHEMATICS CLUB

The Mathematics Club is a student-led organization that fosters a love for mathematics through regular math sessions, workshops, and competitions. Members can learn various math concepts, improve their problem-solving abilities, and develop their logical thinking skills. Join us in calculating, reasoning, and solving our way to math excellence!
CHEMISTRY CLUB

The Chemistry Club is a student-led organization that promotes the study of chemistry among its members. Through regular chemistry sessions, experiments, and competitions, members can enhance their scientific knowledge, improve their analytical skills, and develop their creativity. Join us in mixing, reacting, and discovering our way to chemistry wonders!


Sports Clubs

BADMINTON CLUB

The Badminton Club is a student-led organization that promotes the sport of badminton among its members. Through regular practices, games, and tournaments, members can enhance their physical fitness, teamwork abilities, and badminton skills. Join us in serving, smashing, and scoring our way to victory!
VOLLEYBALL CLUB

The Volleyball Club is a student-led organization that fosters a love for volleyball among its members. Through regular practices, games, and tournaments, members can develop their athleticism, teamwork, and leadership skills. The club provides an opportunity for individuals to enjoy the thrill of competition while improving their volleyball abilities. Join us in serving, spiking, and blocking our way to victory!
BASKETBALL CLUB

The Basketball Club is a student-led organization that aims to promote basketball among its members. Through regular practices, intramural games, and competitive tournaments, members can enhance their physical fitness, leadership abilities, and basketball skills. The club provides an opportunity for individuals to have fun, make new friends, and compete at various levels. Join us in dribbling, shooting, and scoring our way to success!


Home Economic Clubs

SEWING CLUB

The Sewing Club is a student-led organization that teaches members the art of sewing through regular workshops, projects, and competitions. Members can learn various sewing techniques, improve their creativity, and develop their hand-eye coordination. Join us in stitching, threading, and creating our way to fashion!
GARDENING CLUB

The Gardening Club is a student-led organization that fosters a love for gardening through regular gardening sessions, workshops, and competitions. Members can learn various gardening techniques, improve their environmental awareness, and develop their patience and perseverance. Join us in planting, watering, and growing our way to nature's beauty!
COOKING & BAKING CLUB

The Cooking Club is a student-led organization that celebrates the art of cooking through regular cooking sessions, food tastings, and competitions. Members can learn various recipes, improve their culinary skills, and develop their teamwork abilities. Join us in chopping, mixing, and cooking our way to deliciousness!


Contests and Activities

INTER-CAMPUS SCIENTIFIC FEUD

All Canterbury High School Campuses are invited to participate in the "INTER-CAMPUS SCIENTIFIC FEUD" wherein selected students may get the chance to fight other participants of other campuses in a battle of wits in the field of sciences. Students will debate about an assigned topic, or answer quizbees. The winning contestant will recieve a cash prize of 10,000Php as well as to their assigned adviser.

COOK OFF

At Canterbury High School, the cook-off is an annual event where students show off their cooking skills. In the school's kitchen, they create a three-course meal using surprise ingredients. Judges taste and score based on presentation, flavor, and originality. The winner gets a trophy and a chance to compete regionally. It's a fun and friendly competition that brings students together.

SPORTS FEST

Canterbury High School's annual Sports Fest is a thrilling three-day event filled with excitement and friendly competition. Students come together to showcase their skills in three popular sports: badminton, volleyball, and basketball. The festivities kick off with badminton, where players from different houses compete in singles and doubles matches. Next up is volleyball, where teams battle it out on the court in a round-robin tournament. The final day of the fest is dedicated to basketball, where students play in a knockout tournament to determine the ultimate champions. The entire school community comes out to cheer on their favorite players and houses, making this event a highlight of the school year.

BOOK FAIR

Canterbury High School's annual Book Fair is a beloved tradition that brings students and teachers together to celebrate the joy of reading. The fair is filled with an extensive collection of books for all ages and interests, from classic literature to bestsellers, biographies, and graphic novels. The school's library transforms into a bustling marketplace, with students eagerly browsing the shelves, chatting with librarians, and making their selections. The fair also features author visits, book signings, and reading sessions by popular authors. It's a wonderful opportunity for students to discover new books, connect with fellow readers, and foster a love for learning.
